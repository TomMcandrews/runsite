RUN: Radical Underground Neutralization

RUN is a top-down stealth action game set in a dark yet familiar urban environment that tasks the player with escaping from a mazelike warehouse full of dangers. Inspired by stealth classics of the past like Metal Gear Solid and Splinter Cell we aim to deliver an immersive and thrilling expirience for the player as they explore a multitude of dark and decrepit environments on their route to safety.
